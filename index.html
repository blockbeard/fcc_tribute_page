Tribute Page

For my Free Code Camp course one of the projects I needed to do was a tribute page. Their example was lovely with a tasteful page giving details of the life of one of the authors personal heroes. I decided to take a different path.

Inspired by the Tenacious D song "Tribute" I decided to make a "Tribute" page.
